About VideoApp

VideoApp is a video streaming platform that allows users to upload, watch, and share video content across various categories. Inspired by popular video platforms, VideoApp aims to provide a seamless and enjoyable viewing experience for all users.

  • Upload videos up to 100MB in formats like MP4, AVI, and MOV.
  • Stream high-quality videos with easy navigation and search functionality.
  • Browse content based on categories and discover new videos.

This project was developed by Marc Lyndon, a passionate full-stack developer with experience in building scalable web applications using Angular and .NET Core.
